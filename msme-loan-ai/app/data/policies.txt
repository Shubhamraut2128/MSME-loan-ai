MSME Loan Eligibility Rules:

1. Business must be registered in India.
2. Applicant age must be between 21 and 60 years.
3. Business must have minimum 1 year of operation.
4. CIBIL score should be above 650.
5. Annual turnover should be above 5 lakh INR.
6. Udyam Registration is preferred.
7. GST registration required for loans above 10 lakh.
